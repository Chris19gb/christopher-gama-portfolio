Groundwater Potential Zone Mapping

Geospatial Analysis

Project Overview

Conducted groundwater potential zone mapping for Chikwawa district, Malawi using Python, Google Earth Engine, and QGIS to identify areas with high groundwater potential for sustainable water resources management.

Data Integration

  • Digital Elevation Model (DEM) analysis for terrain characteristics
  • Land Use Land Cover (LULC) classification
  • Weather and climate data integration
  • Soil type and geological data analysis
  • Existing well data validation

Methodology

  • Multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA)
  • Analytical Hierarchy Process (AHP) for weighting
  • GIS overlay analysis for potential zone delineation
  • Validation with field data and existing wells

Output

  • Groundwater potential map of Chikwawa district
  • Identification of high, medium, and low potential zones
  • Recommendations for sustainable groundwater development
  • Support for water resources planning and management
